## 5. Strength — Gym, Bodyweight + Basic Barbell + Machines

Simplified from the old plan per your update: fewer exotic movements, more bodyweight, basic barbell lifts, basic machines. 2x/week, ~40 min. Priority: fix weak arms + weak core, support running (glutes, hips, single-leg stability).

**Session A (Mon) — Lower + Push**
- Barbell Back Squat 3×8-10
- Bodyweight/assisted Push-ups (or machine chest press) 3×10-12
- Bodyweight Lunges 2×10/leg
- Plank 3×30-60s

**Session B (Thu) — Pull + Core + Posterior Chain**
- Barbell Deadlift (conventional or RDL) 3×8
- Machine Seated Row or Assisted Pull-up 3×10-12
- Barbell Bench Press 3×10 (your stated favourite — kept)
- Dead Bug 3×8/side
- Glute Bridge (bodyweight or barbell) 3×12-15

**After Session B — Flexibility circuit (12 min):** hip flexor stretch, hamstring stretch, calf stretch, pigeon pose, cat-cow, thoracic rotation — 60s each, carried over from your existing routine, still valid.

**Progression:** add 5% load every 2 weeks on squat/deadlift/bench while form holds. Arms/core: add reps or hold time weekly, not load, until pattern is solid.
